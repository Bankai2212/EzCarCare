Courses Provided:

Information Technology

Information Communication Technology (ICT) is the convergence of Information Technology, Telecommunications and Data Networking Technologies. It is a broad subject concerned with technology and other aspects of managing and processing information. IT covers a multitude of jobs in processing, analysing, storing and conveying information, whether working on the internet or on the systems for guiding rockets. Graduates with work experience and strong technical skills will be snapped up first. Jobs in this area remain a significant feature of graduate recruitment, and the long-term outlook is positive in global terms. There is a growth in e-commerce, mobile computing, netwroking, and digital media and the use of databases.

MOBILE COMPUTING (ANDROID/ IOS)

Mobile Computing is the FUTURE!! The latest developments in mobile computing provides the ability for users to communicate via social applications and games, at their own pace. Although mobile devices, applications, platforms, games and communication are changing rapidly and constantly, our Mobile Computing course enables you to explore the technology through a grounding in computing basics with mobile networking concepts, and focuses on mobile and portable devices such as tablets and mobile phones (iOS, Android and Windows Mobile platform). With the skills learned, you can apply your ideas to develop new and exciting mobile applications for existing and emerging hardware.

BUSINESS INFORMATION MANAGEMENT

Business Information Management offers a thorough understanding of the issues involved in developing and managing information systems in a contemporary business context, particularly the extracting and interpreting of data. This is the most "technical" area in BIM - smart intelligence for smarter decision. Besides, BIM provides knowledge on the technologies used in information management, such as online database systems, data warehousing and business intelligence and artificial intelligence techniques in information management. The programme is geared towards producing graduates whose roles are to plan, coordinate and design business intelligent-related activities in order to fulfill the strategic needs of the organizations in which they are based.
